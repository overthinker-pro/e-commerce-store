The Horo Legacy

Founded on Passion

Horo was born from a singular vision: to create timepieces that are both a joy to behold and a marvel of engineering. Our journey began in a small workshop, driven by a passion for the intricate dance of gears and springs that measures the passage of time.

Today, we honor that heritage by combining traditional watchmaking techniques with modern innovation. Every Horo watch is a piece of art, crafted to be passed down through generations. We are not just selling watches; we are curating legacies.
